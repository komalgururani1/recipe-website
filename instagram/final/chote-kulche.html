Chole kulche

Chole Kulche is a popular North Indian street food dish consisting of spicy and tangy chickpea curry (chole) served with soft and fluffy bread (kulche). It's a flavorful and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Here's a recipe to make Chole Kulche:

prep time

45 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast Snacks food

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and oil or ghee. Mix well. Add yogurt and gradually add water while kneading the dough until it is soft and pliable. Knead for a few minutes to make it smooth. Cover the dough and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

step 2

After resting, divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls. Roll out each ball into an oval or round shape, about ¼ inch thick. Heat a griddle or tawa on medium heat. Place the rolled kulcha on the hot griddle.

step 3

After resting, divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls. Roll out each ball into an oval or round shape, about ¼ inch thick. Heat a griddle or tawa on medium heat. Place the rolled kulcha on the hot griddle.

step 4

Cook for a minute or until bubbles start to appear on the surface. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown spots appear. Remove from the griddle and brush with ghee or butter.

ingredients For making Chole

1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cans of cooked chickpeas)
2 medium onion
2 medium tomatoes
2 green chilies, slit
2 teaspoons chole masala powder
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste
Salt to taste
Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
Cooking oil

ingredients For making Kulche

2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon oil or ghee
½ cup plain yogurt
Salt to taste
Water (as needed)

tools

Griddle or Tawa

Pressure Cooker or Pot

Tongs or Slotted Spoon

Knife and Cutting Board

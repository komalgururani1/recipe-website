Vegetable Soup

Vegetable soup is a nutritious and comforting dish made by combining various vegetables in a flavorful broth. It is a versatile soup that can be customized according to personal taste and the vegetables available. Vegetable soup is not only delicious but also a great way to incorporate a wide range of vegetables into your diet.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast Snacks food

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

step 2

Add the diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, zucchini, and green beans to the pot. Sauté for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften. Pour in the vegetable broth or stock and diced tomatoes (including the juice) into the pot. Stir well to combine.

step 3

Add the dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir again to distribute the seasonings. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the soup simmer for about 15-20 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

step 4

Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the vegetable soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley or cilantro.

ingredients

2 tablespoons cooking oil

1 medium onion, diced

2 cloves of garlic, minced

2 carrots, diced

2 celery stalks, diced

1 bell pepper, diced

1 zucchini, diced

1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into small pieces

4 cups vegetable broth or stock

1 can diced tomatoes (14 ounces)

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

tools

Large Pot or Dutch Oven

Optional: Immersion Blender or Blender

Tongs or Slotted Spoon

Knife and Cutting Board

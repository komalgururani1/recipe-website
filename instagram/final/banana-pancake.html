Banana pancake

Banana pancakes are a delicious and easy-to-make breakfast option. They are made with ripe bananas, flour, and other basic ingredients. Here's a simple recipe for making banana pancakes:

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast Snacks food

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, flour, sugar (if using), baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well until all the ingredients are incorporated. Add the milk and vanilla extract to the banana mixture. Stir until you have a smooth batter. The consistency should be thick but pourable. If the batter is too thick, you can add a little more milk.

step 2

Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease the pan with butter or cooking oil to prevent sticking. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the heated pan. Use the back of the ladle or a spoon to spread the batter into a round shape.

step 3

Cook the pancake until bubbles start to form on the surface, which usually takes about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake using a spatula and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.

step 4

Remove the pancake from the pan and repeat the process with the remaining batter, greasing the pan as needed. Serve the banana pancakes warm with your choice of toppings such as sliced bananas, maple syrup, honey, chopped nuts, or berries.

ingredients

2 ripe bananas, mashed

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon sugar (optional)

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup milk (or any non-dairy milk for a vegan option)

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Butter or cooking oil for greasing the pan

Optional toppings: sliced bananas, maple syrup, honey, chopped nuts, or berries

tools

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Fork or Potato Masher

Non-Stick Skillet or Griddle

Butter or Cooking Oil
